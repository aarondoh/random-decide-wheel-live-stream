Create Action in TikFinity

In TikFinity, go to Actions & Events page:

  1. Click "Create new Action"
  2. Give it a name (e.g., "Add to Wheel")
  3. Scroll down and enable "Trigger Webhook"
  4. Paste your webhook URL in the left textbox
  5. Click "Save"
4

Create Event in TikFinity

Still on the Actions & Events page:

  1. Click "Create new Event"
  2. Under "Define what should trigger this event", select "Gift"
  3. Choose the specific gift type (e.g., Rose, Galaxy)
  4. Under "Trigger all of these actions", select your webhook action
  5. Click "Save"
